QUEER NORDIC CINEMA

about

Queer Nordic Cinema (QNC) is a curatorial collective that programs queer short film events by people from, or based, in the Nordic region.

Founded in 2023, QNC celebrates creativity that challenges the cis- and hetero-gaze in film. We curiously raise questions about the past, present and future through a queer framework. Tired of consuming US- and UK-based queer media, our programming also seeks to uplift local talent from the Nordics.
